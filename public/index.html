What We Offer

Comprehensive Dental Services

From preventive care to specialized treatments, we provide everything your smile needs under one roof.

Dental Radiographs

Digital X-ray imaging for accurate diagnosis of hidden tooth decay, bone loss, and jaw issues.

Oral Surgery

Safe and precise surgical procedures performed under professional care and sterile conditions.

Veneers

Thin porcelain or composite shells bonded to the front of teeth for a natural, polished smile.

Tooth Sealant

Protective coating applied to back teeth to prevent cavities and decay in hard-to-reach areas.

Fluoride Treatment

Professional fluoride application to strengthen enamel and reduce the risk of tooth decay.

Braces / Orthodontic Treatment

Comprehensive teeth alignment solutions including metal braces and orthodontic planning.

Tooth Extraction

Safe and comfortable extractions performed with precision and care when necessary.

Dental Restoration

Restoring damaged or decayed teeth with durable, natural-looking composite materials.

Crowns / Caps

Custom-made dental crowns to protect, cover, and restore the shape of a damaged tooth.

Fixed Partial Dentures (FPD)

Permanent bridge restorations that fill gaps left by missing teeth for a complete bite.

Dentures

Removable full or partial dentures crafted for comfort, function, and a natural appearance.

Oral Prophylaxis / Cleaning

Professional scaling and polishing to remove plaque, tartar, and surface stains effectively.

Root Canal Therapy (RCT)

Effective treatment to save infected teeth and relieve severe toothache by removing damaged pulp.

Oral Check-up

Thorough examination of your teeth, gums, and mouth to maintain long-term oral health.
